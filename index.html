SORT VISUALIZER

Choose an algorithm and see how it will sort the rectangles by its length.

Quicksort

Efficiency: Very high

Chooses an element called pivot. Makes two sublists:
1. Elements greater than pivot
2. Elements lower than pivot.
Repeats for all sublists until sublists have a length of 1.

Bubblesort

Efficiency: Bad

Goes from left to right through the list and compares two elements.
Swaps the two if the left element is greater than the right.
Repeats the process until sorted.

Selectionsort

Efficiency: Medium

Searches the lowest element and places it into the most left position. Then searches the 2nd lowest element and puts it into the 2nd most left position. Repeats in same pattern until sorted.

INFO

The elements are stored in a list. There are two types of operations that can be done:

Compare: Selects two elements and compares their height.
Swap: Selects two elements and swaps their positions in the list
